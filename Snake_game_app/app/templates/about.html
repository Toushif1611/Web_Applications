About the Snake Game

Hello {{ session.get('username', 'Guest') }}!

This application is designed to help players enjoy the classic Snake game.

Features include:

  • Classic Snake gameplay.
  • Easy access to your study materials from anywhere.
  • Secure and private storage for your notes.

Controls:

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to move the snake.
  • Press P to pause and return to the menu.
  • Press R to restart the game.
  • Eat red food blocks to grow. Avoid colliding with your own tail.
  • The game wraps at the edges.

Our goal is to provide a simple and efficient way for players to enjoy the classic Snake game and enhance their gaming experience.
